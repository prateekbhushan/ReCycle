♻️ ReCircle
A mobile-first web prototype for a Circular Economy & Zero-Waste Lifestyle Hub. Built as a single-file HTML/CSS/JS prototype for final year project demonstration.
What It Does
ReCircle helps users track waste, find recycling resources, scan products for sustainability scores, and connect with a community of eco-conscious individuals.
Features
Waste Tracker — Log daily waste by category with quantity, weight, and disposal method
Recycling Map — Find nearby recycling centers with filters and directions
Barcode Scanner — Scan products to check recyclability and get eco-alternatives
Community Feed — Share tips, join challenges, swap items, and celebrate milestones
Gamification — Earn points, unlock badges, maintain streaks, and level up
Impact Dashboard — Visualize personal environmental savings (waste diverted, CO₂ avoided, water saved)
